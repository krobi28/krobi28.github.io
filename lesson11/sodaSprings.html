Soda mothers bring in renowned internet safety speaker

SODA SPRINGS — In the course of a day, a group of local mothers solicited enough donations from throughout their community to cover most of the cost of bringing in a renowned internet safety speaker.

Collin Kartchner, of the Salt Lake City area, will speak during assemblies at Caribou County schools on Wednesday, before addressing parents during a free presentation at 7 p.m. at the Gem Valley Performing Arts Center in Grace.

In the morning, Caribou County’s middle school students will head to Grace to hear him speak, and he’ll address high school students in Soda Springs during the afternoon.

“He speaks to teens about social media and the negative influences of social media,” said Georgia Brown, who is the mother of two elementary school students in Soda Springs.....
