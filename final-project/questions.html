Frequently Asked Questions

  • Which trip is right for me and my family/ group?


    The three basic adventures are just a starting point for your perfect vacation. Please contact us via e-mail or give us a call so we can create a custom adventure to suite your needs.

  • What if I can't swim?


    Here at Salmon River Adventures safety is our number one priority. We provide Coast Gaurd approved life vests as well as helmets. All of our equipment is maintained to the highest standards. If you are ucomfortable with your swimming level, please consider if these precautions are enough to put your fears at ease.

  • Can I take pictures on the river?


    You are welcome to bring your camera to take pictures, but please be aware that it will get wet and Salmon River Adventures does not assume responsibility for damaged or lost items. Please know that we have profesional equipment and photographers staged and ready to snap the perfect shot of your adventure. We highly recommend that you leave the photography to us and just enjoy your trip.

  • Where are you located?


    We are happy to call Riggins, Idaho home. Located on a bend of the Salmon River in the panhandle of Idaho, we enjoy beutiful summer weather, perfect for rafting. Highway 95 runs through Riggins as our Main Street and you can find us at 1000 S Main Street. We look forward to meeting you.

  • What is your cancelation policy?


    We understand that your plans can change for a number of different reasons. If that is the case, please let us know as soon as you can. If your trip is more than 30 days away, we can refund your total amount minus the deposit. If your trip is scheduled in 30 days to 14 days from the cancelation, we can refund 50% of the total cost. Cancelations within 14 days will not be eligable for a refund.

  • What is your privacy policy?


    Salmon River Adventures thanks our loyal customers, and we do so by takeing precautions to make sure your private information stays secure. We keep our technology updated to provide you with certainty that safety and security is our priority. Before your trip begins you will be asked to sign a photography waiver that will allow you to specify how you would like us to handle your photos. It is your choice to make, and we will honor it.

  • Do you have another question?


    We are happy to answer any additional questions you have. Please visit our "Contact Us" page where you can submit your unique question, as well as find our email, or phone number.
