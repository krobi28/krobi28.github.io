Find your perfect river rafting trip. We have something for everyone!

Beginners

Our beginning level trip is a 1 day trip that is promised to be an adventurous day for everyone. Minimum age requirement is 4 years old. There will be time to float as well as swim in the river. Expect to get wet & bring your sun protection. We will provide equipment, lunch and drinks.

Starting at $80

Book Now

Mid Level

Our mid level trip is a 2 day trip that teach you how to work as a team while you enjoy the river and the company. Minimum age requirement is 12 years old. We will camp in a designated camp ground at night. Bring your camping gear and we will make sure it gets to camp dry. Expect to get wet & bring your sun protection. Food and sleeping cabins are provided.

Starting at $200

Book Now

Advanced Level

Our most advanced level adventure is defined by the river that we run, and the time of year. This 2 day adventure runs on the difficult part of the Salmon River that requires a higher skill level. Minimum age requirement is 12 years old. Experience the thrill that only the river can provide as you work together to acheive a common goal. Don't worry about the details, just come prepared ready to enjoy the excitment.

Starting at $250
