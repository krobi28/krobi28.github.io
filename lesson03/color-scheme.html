Color Selection

The color scheme for this weather website can be found in the earth tones all around us. The dark blue is indicative of the night sky, while the olive green and the khaki both are found in plants everywhere. The two purple colors are found often in the shadow of nature just as the song "America the Beautiful" tells of "purple mountain majesty".

Why Select These Colors?

This is not the usual color palate for a weather website, and that is by design. I am hoping to create something different than all the other sites available today which is why I worked to make sure the colors flow and blend well with each other and nature.
